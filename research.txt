technical analysis research notes

indicators to implement:
- rsi (relative strength index): momentum oscillator 0-100
  oversold < 30, overbought > 70
  wilder smoothing method preferred over simple

- macd (moving average convergence divergence):
  macd line = ema(12) - ema(26)
  signal line = ema(9) of macd line
  histogram = macd - signal
  crossovers generate buy/sell signals

- bollinger bands:
  middle = sma(20)
  upper = middle + 2*stdev
  lower = middle - 2*stdev
  squeeze = low bandwidth, expansion coming

- atr (average true range):
  true range = max(h-l, abs(h-prev_c), abs(l-prev_c))
  atr = ema or wilder smooth of true ranges
  useful for position sizing and stop losses

strategy ideas:
- mean reversion: buy when price touches lower bollinger
- momentum: buy on macd crossover with rsi confirmation
- gap plays: look for gap-up with volume confirmation
- 52-week breakouts: price near 52wk high with volume

data considerations:
- yahoo finance has free ohlcv data
- need to handle splits and dividends
- watch out for weekend/holiday gaps
- use adjusted close for accurate returns

volume analysis:
- volume sma(20) as baseline for normal volume
- volume surge = current vol > 1.5x vol_sma
- combine with price breakout above sma for momentum signals
- gap percent measures overnight moves from prev close to open

signal format (shared across strategies):
- every signal dict must have: date, price
- strategy-specific fields are optional extras
- gapup adds: rsi, macd_hist, gap
- movo adds: volume, vol_ratio, rsi
- nobr adds: rsi
- mobr adds: type
- output format: "  {date} ${price:.2f} [extras]"
- consistent print loop and count at the end
